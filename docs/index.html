About

Hi, I'm Jessica Waldron.

I'm a multi-pen-name romance author and AI educator. I built The Invisible Pen because the authors I kept meeting — smart, seasoned storytellers — were being told AI was either magic or cheating, and nobody was showing them the honest middle: a tool that amplifies a voice you've spent decades developing.

Everything I teach comes from my own working process — the same prompts, style sheets, and project structures I use across my own pen names. If it doesn't survive contact with a real manuscript, I don't teach it.
