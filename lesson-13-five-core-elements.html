Element 3 — Hook / Concept

One sentence. Why does THIS book stand out from every other book in this genre? If you need two sentences, the hook isn't sharp enough.

Element 4 — Stakes

What is concretely at risk? "She could lose her job" is a stake. "Things get complicated" is not. Make it specific.

Element 5 — Emotional Core

What should the reader FEEL — and why does that feeling matter right now? This is for the reader, not the character.
