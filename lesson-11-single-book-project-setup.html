4 Run Your Test Conversation

Open a new conversation in the project and run these tests. Click the prompts to copy them.

Context Check Pending
Does Claude know the book?
Summarize what you know about this project — the book, the characters, the genre, and the voice. Click to copy
Voice Check Pending
Does the writing sound like your pen name?
Write the opening paragraph of Chapter 1. Don't ask for details — use everything in the project to get started. Click to copy
Structure Check Pending
POV, tense, and constraints followed?
Write a short scene from Chapter 2 where the two leads interact for the first time. Follow all the structural rules in the project. Click to copy
Character Check Pending
Do the characters match your profiles?
Write a paragraph of internal monologue for each lead character. Show me how they think differently from each other. Click to copy
Constraints Check Pending
Are project-specific rules being followed?
Write a chapter ending for any chapter. Make it follow the hook rules. Then tell me which tropes from the project you'd weave into the next chapter and why. Click to copy

5 Confirm & Launch

Final check before your project is officially ready for writing.

All required knowledge files uploaded
Voice skill, outline, and character profiles are in the project
All 5 tests passed (or fixes made)
Context, voice, structure, character, and constraints all verified
Current status is accurate in instructions
Reflects where you actually are in the manuscript right now

Project Ready

Your single book project is set up and tested. Start a new conversation in the project and begin writing — Claude already knows the book.
