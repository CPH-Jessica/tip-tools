Character Tracker

Lesson 12 — A living document for series continuity

How to use this tracker

Add every named character in your series — leads, supporting, recurring background, even one-scene mentions you might bring back. Update the Last Known Status field after every book. That's the field everyone forgets and the one that prevents 90% of continuity errors.

Your work auto-saves to this browser. When you're ready to feed it to Claude, hit Export for Claude at the bottom — it produces a clean markdown file you upload as a knowledge file in your series project.

When you're ready

Export your tracker as markdown to upload as a knowledge file in your Claude series project — or as JSON for backup and importing into a different browser later.
