### HEAT LEVEL

**Reference: The Heat Level Scale**

| Heat Level | Description | Reader Expectation |
|---|---|---|
| **1 - Sweet/Closed Door** | No sex on page, kissing & emotional intimacy | Romance without sexual content |
| **2 - Warm** | Sexual content implied or fades to black | Sensuality without explicit scenes |
| **3 - Hot** | Explicit scenes, multiple throughout | Clear descriptions, integral to story |
| **4 - Very Hot** | Explicit content frequent & integral | Spicy, often part of character arc |
