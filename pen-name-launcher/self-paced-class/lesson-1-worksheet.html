Next Steps

  1. Review your foundation. Read through all your answers. Do they feel authentic? Does your pen name align with your genre, voice, and reader?
  2. Refine as needed. You can come back and edit any section. There's no "right" answer—only what works for YOU.
  3. Gather your assets. Start collecting color swatches, font samples, and logo sketches. Build a visual mood board.
  4. Test your pen name. Say it out loud. Does it roll off the tongue? Would you be proud to see it on a book cover?
  5. Move to Lesson 2. Once you're confident in your pen name identity, you're ready to build your author platform and establish your digital presence.
