Platform Comparison

Review these three popular platforms and select which fits your needs.

Mailerlite

Free tier: Up to 1,000 subscribers
Price after free: Starting at $25/month (for 1,001-5,000 subscribers)
Interface: Intuitive, creator-friendly, excellent templates

Kit (formerly ConvertKit)

Free tier: Up to 1,000 subscribers
Price after free: Starting at $29/month (for 1,001-5,000 subscribers)
Interface: Creator-focused, excellent for writers, strong automation

Mailchimp

Free tier: Up to 500 contacts
Price after free: Starting at $20/month (for 501-1,000 contacts)
Interface: Feature-rich but steeper learning curve, better for established lists
