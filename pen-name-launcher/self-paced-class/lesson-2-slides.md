## SLIDE 21: Do's and Don'ts
**Title:** "Swap Rules: Do's & Don'ts"
**Content:**

**DO:**
- Swap with authors in your genre
- Swap with similar-sized lists
- Swap with authors you respect
- Track your results
- Do 20-30 swaps in first year

**DON'T:**
- Swap outside your genre (low conversion)
- Swap with much larger authors (lower ROI)
- Sacrifice your reputation for a swap
- Expect massive conversions immediately
- Give up too soon

**Visual:** Checkmarks and X marks
**Style:** Clear, actionable rules
