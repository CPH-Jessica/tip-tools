How Skills Fit In

Click each layer to see what it does. You built Layer 1 last week. This week you're building Layer 2.

Layer 3: Projects (Week 3) A workspace where skills and context come together
Projects are dedicated workspaces in Claude's desktop app. You attach skills, upload manuscripts, and everything stays organized. One project for each pen name or book. Coming in Week 3.
Layer 2: Skills (This Week) Instructions that teach Claude how to do specific jobs
A skill is a document that tells Claude how to do one thing really well — write in your voice, give you honest feedback, edit your chapters. Without skills, Claude gives generic help. With skills, it gives help that matches your brand.
Layer 1: Preferences (Week 1) Claude knows who you are and how you work
Your personal preferences from Week 1. Claude knows your personality, your communication style, and your basic rules. This is the foundation everything else builds on. If you skipped Week 1, go back.
